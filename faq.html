FAQ

Quick answers about events, workshops, points, and how Project Thrive works.

How do I sign up for an event? ›
Go to Events or Workshops, click Sign up, and fill in your name + email. If you’re signed in, your details may auto-fill.
Can I edit my details after signing up? ›
Yes — before you confirm, you can edit your name and email in the sign-up popup. If you made a mistake after confirming, use Contact Us with the event name + time.
How long are workshops? ›
Most workshops are designed to be short and youth-friendly (usually 10–20 minutes).
How do points and streaks work? ›
Points are earned by completing activities (events/workshops/quizzes). Streaks reward consistency over time. You can use points to redeem rewards in the Rewards page.
When do rewards update? ›
Rewards may update weekly depending on availability and partners. Check the Rewards page.
Do I need an account to join? ›
You can sign up without an account by typing your name/email. Creating an account helps with auto-fill, tracking progress, and rewards.
Still need help? Send us a message on the Contact Us page.
